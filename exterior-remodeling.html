Time for a New Look?

Does your roof or siding leak? Is the outside of your home in disrepair? Maybe it's just time for a new look. Whatever it is, Kile Construction will take care of everything.

Homeowners and property owners in the Coeur d'Alene area know how brutal the 4 seasons can be on the outside of a home. There is no project too big or too small for us and we relish the opportunity to help you make the home just the way you want it.

Over 20 Years Of Experience

Chuck and Norm Kile have been doing remodeling in the Coeur d'Alene area for over 20 years. We've seen it all and have built kitchens, bathrooms and additions of all shapes and sizes. We know what styles sell well around here and what features will fetch the highest price. We also know the ins and outs of local regulations and permitting processes which can be quite a headache if you don't know what you're doing.

We can take your plans and drawings, scope out the work, and give you an estimate for a complete project. If you don't have a design or any drawings then we will help you come up with one so we can get you through the process and build the home you really want.

Kile Construction is a full-service residential general contractor so we can do anything from changing a countertop to building a whole new house. With a team like this it's like having your own personal builder ready to customize your house exactly to your liking.
