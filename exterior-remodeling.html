FAQ

Exterior Remodeling Questions

Straight answers to what homeowners ask most before starting an exterior project.

How long does an exterior remodel take?
Most exterior remodels run 2–8 weeks depending on scope. Siding, roofing, deck builds, and window packages each run on different timelines, and we schedule around Idaho weather windows to protect the work.
What siding materials work best in Northern Idaho?
Fiber cement, engineered wood (like LP SmartSide), and quality vinyl all perform well here. We help match siding to the home's style, your budget, and how it will hold up to our freeze-thaw cycles and snow loads.
Can you repair a leaking roof or siding, or do we need a full replacement?
It depends on the condition. Many leaks are localized and can be repaired properly. If the underlying material is failing across the surface, replacement is usually the better long-term decision. We give an honest assessment during the consultation.
Do you build or rebuild decks?
Yes. We build new decks, replace failing decks, and rework railings, stairs, and substructure. Composite and traditional wood options are both available, and structural decks are permitted and inspected.
Do you handle permits for exterior projects?
Yes. We pull the required Idaho or Washington permits and schedule inspections for any work that needs them — including structural decks, additions, and major siding or roofing jobs.
