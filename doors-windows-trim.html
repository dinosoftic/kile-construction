Doors, Windows, and Trim Carpentry in Coeur d'Alene, ID

Small carpentry details can make a big difference in how your home looks, feels, and functions. Kile Construction provides door installation, window replacement for smaller jobs, trim work, and finish carpentry throughout the Coeur d'Alene area.

Whether you are replacing worn components or upgrading the look of a room, we help make the finished result feel clean, complete, and well-built.

Skilled Carpentry Work for Everyday Home Improvements

Doors that do not close properly, outdated trim, damaged window frames, and unfinished details can make a home feel worn out. Good carpentry solves those problems and improves the look of the entire space.

We help homeowners take care of these details with practical recommendations and quality workmanship.

These Details Change the Feel of a Home

A new door can improve privacy, function, and appearance. Updated trim can make an older room feel more finished. Better carpentry can tie a remodel together and make everything look intentional instead of patched together. These are the kinds of upgrades homeowners notice every day.

Decades of Experience With Real Woodworking and Finish Work
