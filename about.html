Our Story

Three decades of remodeling Northern Idaho homes.

Kile Construction was founded over three decades ago and has been the go-to general contractor for homeowners across the Coeur d'Alene area ever since. Norm and Chuck Kile — a father-and-son team — have been building and remodeling homes together for over 30 years, and they haven't slowed down one bit.

We're a full-service residential general contractor. That means one team handles everything from a countertop swap to a whole-house rebuild — it's like having your own personal builder ready to customize your home exactly the way you want it.

"The reason our customers love us is because we are honest craftsmen on a mission to provide great experiences when it comes to building and remodeling homes."

— Norm Kile, Owner

Meet The Team

The Family Behind The Company

A father-and-son team dedicated to quality craftsmanship since day one — the same two names you'll talk to from estimate through final walkthrough.

Owner & Lead Contractor

Norm Kile

Norm has been building and remodeling homes across Northern Idaho for more than 30 years. He founded Kile Construction on a simple promise: honest craftsmanship, clear pricing, and homes built the right way — no shortcuts.

Co-Owner & Craftsman

Chuck Kile

Chuck has been working alongside Norm for decades, leading jobsites and managing the details that separate a good remodel from a great one. From tile layout to finish carpentry, he's hands-on every step of the way.
