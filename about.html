The Family Behind the Company

A father-and-son team dedicated to quality craftsmanship since day one.

Founded over 2 decades ago, Kile Construction is the favorite general contractor for homeowners in the Coeur d'Alene, Idaho area. Norm and Chuck Kile have been building and remodeling homes for over 20 years and they haven't slowed down one bit.

Kile Construction is a full-service residential general contractor so we can do anything from changing a countertop to building a whole new house. With a team like this it's like having your own personal builder ready to customize your house exactly to your liking.
