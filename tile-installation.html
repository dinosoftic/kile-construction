Tile Installation and Backsplashes in Coeur d'Alene, ID

Tile can completely change the look and function of a room when it is installed correctly. Kile Construction provides tile installation in Coeur d'Alene and nearby areas for homeowners who want clean, durable, detail-focused work.

Whether you are updating a kitchen backsplash, replacing bathroom tile, or adding a new finish to a remodel, we help bring the space together the right way.

Tile Work That Adds Style and Holds Up Over Time

Tile work is one of those areas where quality matters immediately. Poor tile installation stands out fast. Uneven lines, bad cuts, loose pieces, and sloppy finishing can ruin the entire look of a kitchen or bathroom.

We take the time to install tile carefully so your finished space looks clean, polished, and built to last.

Our Tile Services Include

  • Kitchen backsplashes
  • Bathroom tile installation
  • Shower tile
  • Fireplace surround tile
  • Tile flooring
  • Accent tile features
  • Small tile repair and replacement, depending on scope

Kitchen Backsplashes

Backsplashes are one of the most popular small kitchen upgrades because they add visual appeal and protect the walls behind sinks, counters, and cooktops. Whether you want something classic, modern, textured, or bold, we can install a backsplash that fits the style of your kitchen and finishes the space properly.

Better Bathroom Finishes Start With Better Installation

Bathrooms take daily wear, constant moisture, and a lot of close-up visibility. Tile needs to look good and perform well. We install bathroom tile with an eye for both appearance and function, helping you create a space that feels clean, updated, and easier to enjoy every day.

Experienced Craftsmanship for Detail-Oriented Work

Not every contractor enjoys doing tile. We do. It is the kind of work that rewards patience, skill, and a good eye, and it is an area where experience shows. If you want your backsplash, shower, or tile upgrade done neatly and professionally, Kile Construction is a strong choice.
