Over 20 Years Of Experience

Chuck and Norm Kile have been remodeling bathrooms in the Coeur d'Alene area for over 20 years. We've seen it all and have built bathrooms of all shapes and sizes. We know what styles sell well around here and what features will fetch the highest price. We also know the ins and outs of local regulations and permitting processes which can be quite a headache if you don't know what you're doing.

We can take your plans and drawings, scope out the work, and give you an estimate for a complete project. If you don't have a design or any drawings then we will help you come up with one so we can get you through the process and build the bathroom you really want.

Kile Construction is a full-service residential general contractor so we can do anything from changing a countertop to building a whole new house. With a team like this it's like having your own personal builder ready to customize your house exactly to your liking.

We Will Inspect Your Home Before Starting

If you have an old home then it's likely been renovated before. We never know exactly what to expect until we open up the walls and see for ourselves, though sometimes we can make a pretty good guess!

If you have an old home, then it's best to have us come out and inspect before you get too far into the process. This is especially true before you start tearing things apart. On occasion homeowners start to demolish their home only to find that they open a can of worms and in order to legally put it back together they have to renovate the entire thing. This can be costly… Can you imagine?

The moral of the story is to have us come out and inspect your home before you get your project underway. It might save you a lot of money and heartache.
