FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what homeowners ask us most often before starting a project.

What areas does Kile Construction serve?
We serve Coeur d'Alene, Hayden, Post Falls, Rathdrum, Dalton Gardens, Hayden Lake, Athol, Spirit Lake, and surrounding Northern Idaho communities. We also take on projects in Spokane, Spokane Valley, and Liberty Lake, Washington.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. We hold Idaho contractor license RCE-34546 and Washington license CC NLKILKC815OZ. We are fully insured and EPA Lead-Safe Certified for work on older homes.
How long does a typical remodel take?
Timelines depend on scope. Bathrooms usually take 2–4 weeks, kitchens 4–8 weeks, basement finishes 4–8 weeks, and whole-home renovations or additions 2–4 months. We'll give you a realistic schedule in writing before work starts.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. We provide free in-home consultations and written, itemized estimates for all remodeling projects in our service area. No pressure, no obligation.
Do I need permits for my remodel?
Most structural, electrical, and plumbing work requires permits. As your general contractor we pull all required permits and schedule inspections — you don't have to deal with the city or county.
Can you help with design and material selections?
Absolutely. We'll walk you through layout options, help source materials, and recommend local suppliers for cabinets, countertops, tile, fixtures, and finishes.
How do I get started?
Call us at (208) 494-8818 or request a meeting online. We'll schedule a free on-site visit, talk through your goals, and put together a written estimate.
